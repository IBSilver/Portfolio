About me

Hi! My name is Iván, and I'm a Junior Game Designer & QA Tester.

For as long as I can remember, I have always loved video games, ever since I had my first Game Boy Advance. After studying computer science, I decided to study video games design & development in order to dedicate myself to what I have always liked.

I'm a person who really likes to document things, whether it's making lists of random things or documenting a new idea I come up with. In general, I like to always have things written down and in order. With all that I have learned and continue to learn, I strive to grow as a person and as a professional.
