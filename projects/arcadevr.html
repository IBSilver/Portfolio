Arcade VR

VR arcade simulation video game

2024 / Producer, Designer

Description

This project consists of creating a simple arcade room simulation experience in VR from scratch.

The project was a 5 member group assignment for the 'Augmented reality' subject in 3rd year of the Degree in Video Game Design and Development at CITM - UPC in Terrassa, Spain.

In this project I was in charge of the production, research and design of the game. I was also in charge of the search for assets and their implementation in the scene.

This project was made in Unity using free assets and own assets.
