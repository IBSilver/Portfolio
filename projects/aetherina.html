Aetherina

Fantasy RPG design concept

2023 / Designer

Description

This project consists of designing and documenting an RPG game, more specifically the worldbuilding and combat.

The project was a group work of 4 members for the subject 'Game Design II' of the 3rd year of the Degree in Game Design and Development at CITM - UPC in Terrassa, Spain.

In this project I was in charge of defining the narrative and its variations according to the player's choices. I was also in charge of defining the different resources that can be collected in the game and the objects that can be made from these resources.

But where I put more focus was on the combat design, stats and attacks of the characters and enemies.

Task summary

  • GDD organization.
  • Narrative design.
  • Items design.
  • Combat design.
  • Characters & enemies stats & attacks.
